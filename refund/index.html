Refund Policy

Digital product refund terms for Wisteria Inbox Suite purchases, including the 14-day refund standard required for Paddle review.

Wisteria Inbox Suite is sold as a digital software product. No physical goods are shipped.

Customers are entitled to request a full refund within 14 days of purchase, in line with Paddle buyer protection standards and applicable consumer obligations.

This policy applies to eligible digital purchases of Wisteria Inbox Suite products sold through Paddle, including annual access plans and related paid software licenses presented on this site.

Customers can request a refund through Paddle using the purchase receipt email, or by contacting support@wisteriasoftware.uk so we can help route the request correctly.

Approved refunds are processed by Paddle and may take 5 to 10 business days to return to the original payment method.

6. Contact

If you need help with a refund request, order lookup, or payment issue, contact support@wisteriasoftware.uk.
