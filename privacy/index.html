Privacy Policy

For Wisteria Inbox Suite products, including ChatGPT inbox, Inbox Capture, and Inbox Local.

Summary

Wisteria products are designed around local-first workflows. We collect limited operational data necessary for payments, security, licensing, support, and service delivery.

1. Products Covered

This policy applies to the Wisteria Inbox Suite product portal, ChatGPT inbox, Inbox Capture, Inbox Local, and related purchase and support flows.

2. Data We May Process

We may process account identifiers, purchase metadata from Paddle, technical request data required for security or diagnostics, and support messages that you send to us.

3. Local-First Content Handling

Inbox Capture processes user-selected page content locally in the browser. If the user enables local integration, the extension may connect to http://127.0.0.1:8124 on the same device to pass content into Inbox Local.

4. AI Settings

When you use custom AI settings, API keys and model settings should be stored locally in your environment or browser context unless a future feature explicitly states otherwise.

5. Payments

Payments are handled by Paddle as Merchant of Record. Paddle may process billing and transaction information under its own privacy and compliance framework.

6. Contact

For privacy questions, contact support@wisteriasoftware.uk.

7. Refund and Payment Context

Related pricing, terms, refund policy, and support information are available alongside the checkout flow so buyers can review them before payment.
